Team 10A

The Problem

Problem Summary

The Curio Robot, an educational platform intended to teach undergraduate students about programming, web development, UI/UX design, and AI/ML using affordable materials. However It is faced with limitations such as:

1

DESIGN CONFUSION

Users often found the robot's design confusing, with no clear indication of which side was the 'front'.

2

SLow speed

The robot's speed was a consistent point of contention.

3

more features

Users expressed a desire for the robot to offer a more dynamic learning experience and for its design to better facilitate project sharing and rapid learning.

The Team Task

Curio Limitations

The team is to redesign the Curio Bot while addressing the its current limitations.

Enhance Learning Experience

The team is to keep in mind that the purpose of the Curio Bot is for Schools and Students to use to enchance their learning process.

Affordability

As the robot is to be used by schools and students, the materials and methods used to make the robot has to be affordable.
